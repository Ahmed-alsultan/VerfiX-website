The VerfiX Vision

The European identity and payment infrastructure of the future

A unified, sovereign, and frictionless ecosystem where verification and transactions happen in an instant — across borders, with the highest standards of privacy and security.

Talk to us Explore the products
Long-term vision — some capabilities described here are concepts not yet available
What the vision means

One continent, one trust layer

Six shifts that turn 27 fragmented systems into a single, seamless economic space.

One continent, one trust layer

Any citizen or business verifies identity and transacts across borders as easily as at home — without the fragmentation of 27 different systems.

Pay with your face and voice

Authentication becomes natural and secure. Look at a camera or speak a phrase — no cards, no PINs, no passwords. Just your biometric signature.

Instant transactions

Verification and settlement in under one second — frictionless for buyers and sellers, enabling real-time commerce across the continent.

Aligned with European laws

Built from the ground up for eIDAS 2.0, GDPR, and Swiss nFADP — legal certainty for every party.

Sovereignty and trust

Data stays within Europe's borders. Infrastructure owned and operated by European institutions — not non-European corporations.

A complete ecosystem

Not just a payment method — a full ecosystem for identity, compliance, fraud prevention, and secure transactions for banks, governments, fintechs, and retailers.

The VerfiX Promise
Where your identity is your key. Where transactions happen in an instant. Where trust is built into the infrastructure — not outsourced to third parties.

01

VerfiX KYC / KYB

"Know your customer. Know your business. Know it's real."

A modular, AI-powered platform for identity verification of individuals and businesses — combining document verification, face and voice biometrics, AML screening, and fraud intelligence into one seamless workflow.

One platform. Every check. Complete trust.

Explore KYC & KYB
Product video coming soon
02

VerfiX Gateway

"One API. Every European identity. Full trust."

The unified gateway for European Digital Identity Wallets (eIDAS 2.0). Banks and regulated organizations connect once — and accept trusted digital identities from all 27 EU member states, plus Switzerland.

No more 27 integrations. Just one gateway.

Explore Gateway
Product video coming soon
03

VerfiX Chip Element

"Your data, your device, your control."

A Swiss-made, ultra-low-power chip (in collaboration with CSEM) that runs face and voice verification on-device — no data ever leaves the chip. Built for ATMs, POS terminals, and secure access points.

Trust, without the cloud.

04

VerfiX SDK

"Build your own trust infrastructure."

A flexible, modular SDK that enables developers and enterprises to embed VerfiX's identity verification, biometric authentication, and compliance workflows directly into their own applications — from mobile apps to web portals to enterprise systems.

Your platform. Our trust. Infinite possibilities.
