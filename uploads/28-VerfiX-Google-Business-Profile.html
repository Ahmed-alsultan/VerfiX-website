Business Description (750 characters maximum)

VerfiX AG is a Swiss identity infrastructure company providing identity verification, business verification, AML screening, and risk scoring for regulated industries. Our platform enables banks, fintechs, insurance companies, telecom operators, and government agencies to verify who they are dealing with, in compliance with FINMA, GwG, and nFADP requirements. All data is processed and stored exclusively in Switzerland. One API. Full audit trail. Swiss data residency guaranteed.

Q&A — Pre-approved Answers

Q: Is VerfiX FINMA compliant?

A: VerfiX is designed to meet FINMA Circular 2016/7 requirements for remote identification including document verification, biometric matching, and liveness detection. Customers should confirm with their compliance counsel.

Q: Does VerfiX keep data in Switzerland?

A: Yes. All processing and storage occurs exclusively in Switzerland. VerfiX does not transfer personal data outside Switzerland.
