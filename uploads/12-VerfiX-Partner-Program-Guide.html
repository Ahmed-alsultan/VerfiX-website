VerfiX Partner Program

The VerfiX Partner Program enables technology companies, system integrators, consulting firms, and channel partners to bring Swiss-compliant identity verification capabilities to their customers and platforms.

Program Overview

VerfiX AG believes that the best way to build the Swiss identity infrastructure layer is through an ecosystem of partners who extend the platform's reach into vertical markets, technology stacks, and customer relationships that VerfiX cannot serve alone.

The Partner Program provides partners with the commercial, technical, and marketing resources to build profitable practices around VerfiX identity verification and compliance capabilities.

Partner Tiers

Tier Criteria Benefits
Referral Partner Active referral relationship. No minimum revenue. Referral commission on introduced customers. Partner portal access. Sales materials.
Solution Partner Integration of VerfiX into own platform or solution. Minimum 3 active customers. Revenue share. Technical support. Co-marketing. Partner badge.
Strategic Partner Significant VerfiX revenue contribution. Joint go-to-market plan. Enhanced revenue share. Dedicated partner manager. Joint press opportunities. Early product access.

Note: Specific commercial terms for each partner tier are defined in the Partner Agreement. Contact contact@verfix.ch to discuss partnership.

Partner Types

Technology Integration Partners

Software companies that integrate VerfiX capabilities into their platforms — core banking systems, CRM platforms, compliance tools, onboarding software, or digital banking applications. Integration is via REST API. VerfiX provides technical support during integration and ongoing API access.

System Integrators

Professional services firms that implement VerfiX as part of larger digital transformation, compliance modernisation, or onboarding platform projects for end customers. System integrators add value through implementation expertise, change management, and integration with complex legacy environments.

Consulting Partners

Advisory firms that recommend VerfiX to their regulated industry clients as part of compliance programme design, RegTech strategy, or digital onboarding advisory engagements. Consulting partners provide referrals and may participate in customer implementation.

Resellers

Organisations that resell VerfiX subscriptions to end customers, typically bundled with their own services or added-value packages. Resellers manage the customer relationship and billing, with VerfiX providing the platform and technical support.
