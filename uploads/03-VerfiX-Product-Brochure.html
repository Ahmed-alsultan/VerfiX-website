Swiss Identity Infrastructure Platform

VerfiX: Swiss Identity Infrastructure

VerfiX is the identity and compliance infrastructure platform built for regulated industries in Switzerland and Europe. One API. Four product modules. All processing in Switzerland.

The Platform

Traditional identity verification is fragmented: one vendor for document scanning, another for biometrics, another for AML screening, and a fourth for risk scoring. Each adds integration cost, data residency risk, and compliance overhead. VerfiX unifies all of these into a single, Swiss-hosted API layer that works with your existing systems.

⬡ Diagram: Single API layer connecting Organisation's systems (Core Banking / CRM / App) to four VerfiX modules (KYC → KYB → eID Gateway → Trust Engine), all sitting on Swiss-hosted infrastructure with a unified Audit Trail beneath

Product 1: KYC — Know Your Customer

Verify the identity of individuals in seconds, fully digitally, with a FINMA-compliant audit trail generated automatically.

Capability Description
Document Verification Reads and validates identity documents from 4,500+ types across 190+ countries. Checks MRZ, security features, and document authenticity.
OCR & MRZ Extraction Optical character recognition and Machine Readable Zone extraction from passports, ID cards, and driving licences.
NFC Chip Reading Reads biometric data from NFC-enabled ePassport chips, providing the highest assurance level of identity verification.
Face Verification Biometric match between live selfie and document photograph. ISO 30107-3 compliant.
Liveness Detection Active and passive liveness detection to prevent spoofing attacks using photographs, videos, or 3D masks.
Voice Biometrics Speaker verification for telephone banking and voice-based authentication. Supports Swiss German, French, Italian, and Romansh.
Audit Trail Immutable, timestamped record of every verification event. FINMA inspection ready. 10-year retention supported.

Regulatory alignment: FINMA Circular 2016/7, GwG Art. 3, nFADP.

Product 2: KYB — Know Your Business

Automated business verification for legal entities, from company registry lookup to UBO identification and sanctions screening.

Capability Description
Company Registry Lookup Automated query of Zefix (CH) and major EU business registries. Returns company identification, directors, and ownership data.
Ownership Structure Mapping Traces ownership chains to natural persons. Generates ownership graph with percentage holdings at each tier.
UBO Identification Identifies all natural persons above the 25% GwG threshold. Flags nominee structures for manual review.
Director Verification KYC-level identity verification for company directors and authorised signatories.
Sanctions & PEP Screening Real-time screening of all identified entities and persons against global sanctions and PEP lists.
GwG-Compliant Audit Trail Structured audit record per KYB check, including company extract, ownership graph, UBO identification, and AML screening results.

Regulatory alignment: GwG Art. 4, FINMA AML guidance, nFADP.

Product 3: eID Gateway

A single API integration that enables Swiss organisations to accept digital identity credentials from EU Digital Identity Wallets across all 27 EU member states. Designed for the eIDAS 2.0 rollout.

  • ›Accept EUDIW credentials without building 27 separate integrations
  • ›Cryptographic credential verification — highest assurance level
  • ›Standardised identity result returned to your onboarding workflow
  • ›Designed to support eIDAS 2.0 obligations as they come into force
  • ›Positioned for Swiss-EU digital identity interoperability agreements

Status: Designed for eIDAS 2.0 rollout timeline. Available for early access and integration planning.

Product 4: Trust Engine

Automated risk scoring, AML screening, and decision intelligence for every customer interaction.

Capability Description
AML Screening Real-time screening against 60+ global lists including SECO, UN, EU, OFAC, and HM Treasury.
PEP Screening Identification of politically exposed persons, their family members, and close associates.
Sanctions Screening Continuous screening against government-maintained sanctions lists with configurable match thresholds.
Risk Scoring Composite Trust Score 0–100 aggregating all verification signals into a single risk indicator.
Fraud Detection Deepfake detection, synthetic identity detection, document fraud signals, and device intelligence.
Decision Rules Configurable automated decision rules: approve, review, or reject based on Trust Score thresholds and signal combinations.
Webhooks Real-time notifications for verification completion, AML hits, and risk threshold breaches.
Ongoing Monitoring Automated rescreening of existing customer base against updated sanctions and PEP lists.
