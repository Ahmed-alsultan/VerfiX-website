Business Verification for Legal Entities

VerfiX KYB: Business Verification

VerfiX KYB automates the verification of legal entities — from company registry lookup to ultimate beneficial owner identification and AML screening — in a single workflow that produces a GwG-compliant audit record.

Executive Summary

Manual KYB processes are the most significant compliance bottleneck for Swiss financial institutions. A single merchant or business onboarding can require multiple registry lookups, ownership chain analysis, director identity verification, and AML screening — a process that can take days when conducted manually.

VerfiX KYB automates the entire workflow, integrating directly with Swiss and EU business registries, mapping ownership structures programmatically, routing identified individuals through the KYC verification flow, and generating a complete GwG-compliant audit trail. What takes three days manually takes under four hours with VerfiX KYB.

Core Capabilities

Capability Detail
Company Registry Lookup Automated query of Zefix (Swiss Federal Commercial Register) for Swiss companies. EU business registry integration for foreign companies. Returns company identification, registration status, registered address, legal form, directors, and ownership.
Ownership Structure Mapping Programmatic construction of ownership graph from registry data. Traces shareholding chains through multiple tiers to identify natural persons. Visualised as ownership tree with percentage holdings at each level.
UBO Identification Identifies all natural persons who meet or exceed the 25% GwG threshold for beneficial ownership. Flags nominee shareholder structures and complex holding arrangements for enhanced due diligence review.
Director Verification KYC-level identity verification for company directors and authorised signatories. Integrates with VerfiX KYC module for document verification and biometric matching.
Sanctions & PEP Screening Automated screening of all identified legal entities and natural persons against global sanctions lists and PEP registries at point of onboarding.
Ongoing Monitoring Continuous rescreening of all entities and persons identified in the KYB process against updated sanctions and PEP lists. Alerts on new matches.
GwG-Compliant Audit Trail Structured audit record per KYB check, including company extract (with date), ownership graph, UBO identification rationale, all screening results, and decision outcome. Supports 10-year retention.

KYB Workflow

⬡ Diagram: KYB Automated Flow — (1) Input: Company name / registration number → (2) Registry lookup (Zefix / EU registry) → (3) Extract directors, shareholders, capital structure → (4) Map ownership chain to natural persons → (5) Flag UBOs above 25% threshold → (6) Route UBOs and directors to KYC flow (document + biometric verification) → (7) Screen all entities and persons against sanctions + PEP → (8) Generate Trust Score → (9) Decision: Approve / Review / Reject → (10) Generate GwG audit trail → (11) Webhook to organisation system

Supported Jurisdictions

VerfiX KYB supports business verification for companies registered in:

  • ›Switzerland: Full Zefix integration for AG, GmbH, Stiftung, Genossenschaft, Verein, and other Swiss legal forms.
  • ›European Union: Major EU business registries for the most common incorporation jurisdictions.
  • ›Other jurisdictions: Manual document-based verification workflow with structured data capture for companies incorporated outside Switzerland and the EU.

Compliance Summary

GwG Requirement How VerfiX KYB Addresses It
Art. 3 — identification of contracting party Company registry lookup provides verified legal name, registration number, address, and legal form. Current extract (timestamped) retained in audit trail.
Art. 4 — beneficial owner identification Automated ownership chain mapping to natural persons. UBOs above 25% flagged and routed to KYC verification. Rationale documented in audit trail.
Art. 7 — documentation Structured audit record per KYB check. All evidence, decisions, and screening results retained. Exportable for FINMA inspection.
Art. 4 para. 2 — senior managing official If no UBO above 25% threshold identified, platform flags for senior managing official identification per GwG requirements.
Art. 13 — increased due diligence Configurable enhanced due diligence workflow for complex ownership structures, high-risk jurisdictions, or elevated risk scores.
