The Problem

Digital identity is proliferating. Complexity is too.

Under eIDAS 2.0, every EU member state must offer a national Digital Identity Wallet by 2026. Each deploys a different ecosystem — different issuers, trust registries, credential formats, revocation mechanisms, and national schemes running in parallel.

This creates 27+ distinct integration targets for any regulated organization operating across borders. Building and maintaining each one individually is not viable — it is infrastructure fragmentation at a European scale.

Different countries, different ecosystems

Spain, Germany, France, Italy — each has its own digital identity infrastructure.

Different trust registries and issuers

No unified source of trust. Each issuer must be individually verified per jurisdiction.

Bespoke integrations per ecosystem

Building separate connectors multiplies cost, maintenance, and compliance risk.

The Solution

Connect once. Verify everywhere.

Connect once to VerfiX Gateway. VerfiX manages verification, trust validation, and interoperability across supported digital identity ecosystems. Your organization gets one clean API — regardless of how many ecosystems are behind it.

Architecture

One Gateway. Every Ecosystem.

VerfiX Gateway sits between digital identity ecosystems and regulated organizations, handling all trust validation and interoperability.

Capabilities

What VerfiX Gateway verifies

Nine verification capabilities delivered through one API.

Wallet Verification

Verify the authenticity and trustworthiness of digital identity wallets before accepting credentials.

Credential Verification

Validate digital credentials against trusted issuer registries and schema standards.

Issuer Verification

Confirm issuers are recognized and trusted within the relevant national ecosystem.

Digital Signature Validation

Verify cryptographic signatures to confirm credential integrity and authenticity.

Trust Registry Verification

Cross-reference credentials against national and European trust registries.

Revocation Status Checks

Confirm that credentials have not been revoked, suspended, or invalidated.

Cross-Border Identity

Verify identities issued in one jurisdiction when presented in another.

Identity Interoperability

Normalize identity data across ecosystems into one consistent output format.

Trust Scoring

Every credential verification produces a trust score via Trust Engine™.
