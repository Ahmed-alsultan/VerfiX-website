1. SEO Strategy Overview

Primary Target Audience

Compliance officers, technology leaders, and procurement teams at Swiss regulated organisations researching identity verification and AML compliance solutions.

Search Intent

Commercial: Evaluating identity verification vendors. Informational: Understanding FINMA, GwG, nFADP requirements. Navigational: Finding VerfiX specifically.

Competitive Positioning

VerfiX competes for search traffic on Swiss-specific compliance terms (FINMA KYC, GwG compliance, nFADP biometric data) where international competitors have limited content depth, and on Swiss data residency terms where VerfiX has a genuine differentiated story.
