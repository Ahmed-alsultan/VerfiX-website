Platform Resilience and Recovery Capabilities

Business Continuity Overview — VerfiX AG

This document describes VerfiX AG's approach to business continuity, platform resilience, and recovery capabilities. It is intended for enterprise customers conducting due diligence on the operational resilience of the VerfiX identity verification platform.

Platform Resilience Architecture

The VerfiX platform is designed for high availability through architectural redundancy at all layers:

Layer Resilience approach
Data centre Primary and secondary Swiss data centres in separate geographic locations. Automatic failover on primary site failure.
Compute Stateless service architecture enables horizontal scaling. No single server is a single point of failure.
Data storage Audit trail and compliance records replicated synchronously to secondary location. No risk of data loss on primary failure.
Network Redundant network paths between data centres and to internet exchange. Single network path failure does not affect service.
API gateway Load-balanced across multiple instances. Automatic rerouting on instance failure.
DNS Multiple DNS providers. Geographic DNS distribution for optimal routing.

Recovery Time and Recovery Point Objectives

Scenario RTO (target) RPO (target)
Single server failure <1 minute (automatic failover) Zero (stateless — no data at risk)
Single data centre failure <15 minutes (DNS failover to secondary) <1 minute (synchronous replication)
Full platform rebuild (catastrophic) <4 hours Less than 24 hours (daily backup restore)
Audit trail corruption N/A — tamper-evident. Corruption detectable. Zero (synchronous replication to secondary)

Note: RTO and RPO targets are design objectives. Specific contractual SLA commitments are defined in the Service Agreement.

Data Backup and Recovery

  • ›Audit trail records: Replicated synchronously to secondary Swiss data centre. Point-in-time recovery possible.
  • ›Configuration data: Backed up daily to secondary location. Encrypted. Tested quarterly.
  • ›Customer data: Subject to retention periods defined in the DPA. Backups are encrypted and access-controlled.
  • ›Recovery testing: Backup restoration tests conducted quarterly. Results documented.

Incident and Outage Response

In the event of a service disruption:

  • ›Automated monitoring detects the disruption and triggers alerts to the on-call operations team within minutes.
  • ›On-call team assesses severity and initiates the appropriate response track per the Incident Response Policy.
  • ›Customers are notified of significant service disruptions via the status page (planned: status.verfix.ch) and direct notification for Critical incidents.
  • ›Resolution is prioritised based on customer impact and severity classification.
  • ›Post-incident review conducted and report made available to affected customers.

Planned Maintenance

Planned maintenance requiring service interruption is:

  • ›Communicated to customers with at least 7 days' advance notice
  • ›Scheduled during periods of low usage (weekends or off-peak hours for the relevant customer timezone)
  • ›Limited in duration to the minimum necessary
  • ›Cancelled or postponed if customer impact assessment indicates unacceptable risk

Vendor and Supply Chain Resilience

  • ›VerfiX maintains alternative options for critical infrastructure components to avoid single-vendor dependency
  • ›Sub-processor resilience is assessed as part of the sub-processor due diligence process
  • ›Critical third-party services (AML list providers, registry APIs) have documented fallback procedures for unavailability

Customer Responsibilities

Enterprise customers can enhance their resilience by:

  • ›Implementing webhook retry logic with exponential backoff in their integration to handle temporary API unavailability
  • ›Designing onboarding workflows with a graceful degradation mode for periods of reduced verification capacity
  • ›Storing verification_id and audit_id immediately on receipt, before proceeding with downstream processing
  • ›Subscribing to VerfiX status notifications (when available) for advance awareness of maintenance windows
