VerfiX AG — Data Protection Notice

Privacy Policy — VerfiX AG

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how VerfiX AG ("VerfiX", "we", "us") collects, processes, and protects personal data in connection with our identity verification platform and website (verfix.ch). It applies to:

  • ›Visitors to verfix.ch
  • ›Individuals who contact VerfiX for product information, demonstrations, or support
  • ›Employees and contractors of organisations that use the VerfiX platform (customer users)

It does not apply to individuals whose identity is verified through the VerfiX platform on behalf of VerfiX's customers. Processing of those individuals' data is governed by the privacy policies of the relevant customer (the data controller) and the Data Processing Agreement between VerfiX and that customer.

1. Identity and Contact Details of the Controller

VerfiX AG is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with the VerfiX website and customer relationship management. Contact for data protection matters:

VerfiX AG | privacy@verfix.ch | verfix.ch

2. Categories of Personal Data Processed

Category Examples Processing purpose
Contact and enquiry data Name, email, company, phone, content of enquiry Responding to enquiries, providing product demonstrations, managing the customer relationship
Customer user account data Name, email, company, job title, dashboard access credentials Providing access to the VerfiX customer dashboard and platform management
Technical and usage data IP address, browser type, pages visited, session duration Website analytics, platform security monitoring, fraud prevention
Communication data Email and call records for support and account management Customer support, compliance with communication obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing activity Legal basis (nFADP)
Responding to enquiries and providing demonstrations Consent (by submitting the contact form) / Legitimate interest in developing the business relationship
Managing customer user accounts Performance of contract (the service agreement between VerfiX and the customer organisation)
Website analytics Legitimate interest in understanding website usage and improving the platform
Platform security monitoring Legitimate interest in protecting the platform and customer data from unauthorised access
Marketing communications (where applicable) Consent (where required) / Legitimate interest (for existing customers)

4. Data Recipients and Sub-Processors

VerfiX AG processes all personal data within Switzerland. A limited number of sub-processors are used for specific functions. All sub-processors are contractually bound to process personal data only for the purposes specified, to apply appropriate security measures, and to comply with applicable data protection law.

A current list of sub-processors is available at verfix.ch/trust-center and will be updated with reasonable advance notice of any material changes.

5. Retention Periods

Data category Retention period
Contact and enquiry data (non-customer) 3 years from last contact, or until erasure requested
Customer user account data Duration of service agreement + 3 years thereafter
Website analytics data 24 months from collection (or shorter if required by applicable law)
Support communication records 3 years from resolution of the relevant support case

6. Data Subject Rights

Under nFADP, individuals whose personal data VerfiX processes as controller have the right to:

  • ›Access: Request information about the personal data VerfiX holds about them
  • ›Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • ›Erasure: Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • ›Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • ›Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • ›Portability: Receive a copy of personal data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@verfix.ch. VerfiX will respond within 30 days. Where a request relates to personal data processed on behalf of a customer (rather than for VerfiX's own purposes), VerfiX will forward the request to the relevant customer as data controller.

7. Cookies and Tracking

VerfiX uses a limited number of cookies on the verfix.ch website:

  • ›Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • ›Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors use the website. Enabled only with consent.

Cookie preferences can be managed through the cookie consent interface on the website. For more information, see the Cookie Policy at verfix.ch/privacy.

8. Security

VerfiX applies appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure, or access. Key measures include AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, role-based access controls, and multi-factor authentication for all administrative access. For further detail, see the VerfiX Security Overview at verfix.ch/trust-center.

9. Changes to This Policy

VerfiX may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to affected individuals by email or by notice on the website. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this document.

10. Contact and Complaints

For questions about this Privacy Policy or the exercise of data subject rights: privacy@verfix.ch

Complaints may be addressed to the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC): edoeb.admin.ch
