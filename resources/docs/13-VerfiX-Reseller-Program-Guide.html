Reselling VerfiX Identity Verification

VerfiX Reseller Program

The VerfiX Reseller Program enables qualified organisations to resell VerfiX identity verification and compliance capabilities to regulated industries across Switzerland and Europe.

What Resellers Sell

Resellers offer VerfiX platform subscriptions to their end customers, typically as part of a broader service offering. The VerfiX products available for resale include:

  • ›KYC: Identity verification for individuals — document verification, biometric matching, liveness detection
  • ›KYB: Business verification — company registry, UBO identification, director verification
  • ›eID Gateway: EU Digital Identity Wallet credential acceptance (planned rollout)
  • ›Trust Engine: AML screening, PEP screening, sanctions screening, risk scoring

Resellers may bundle VerfiX with their own services (compliance consulting, implementation, managed compliance) or resell as a standalone subscription.

Reseller Commercial Model

VerfiX operates a usage-based pricing model. Resellers purchase VerfiX capacity at a wholesale rate and resell to end customers at their own retail price. The margin between wholesale and retail is the reseller's revenue on the VerfiX component.

Key commercial principles:

  • ›Wholesale pricing is agreed in the Reseller Agreement based on committed volume tiers
  • ›Resellers are free to set their own retail pricing to end customers
  • ›Resellers are responsible for billing and collection from end customers
  • ›VerfiX invoices the reseller at the agreed wholesale rate based on actual usage
  • ›Minimum commitment volumes and tiers are defined in the Reseller Agreement

Specific pricing and commercial terms are not published. Contact contact@verfix.ch to discuss reseller pricing.

Reseller Obligations

Resellers who wish to sell VerfiX must:

  • ›Execute the VerfiX Reseller Agreement
  • ›Complete VerfiX product training (provided by VerfiX)
  • ›Ensure end customers execute acceptable use terms consistent with VerfiX's Terms of Service
  • ›Maintain the confidentiality of VerfiX commercial terms with end customers
  • ›Not represent VerfiX capabilities beyond what is described in current VerfiX documentation
  • ›Notify VerfiX of all end customer deployments within 30 days of go-live

VerfiX Obligations to Resellers

  • ›Provide resellers with current product documentation, sales materials, and pricing
  • ›Provide technical support for reseller integration questions
  • ›Provide end customer technical support for platform issues (not reseller commercial issues)
  • ›Maintain platform availability per the agreed SLA
  • ›Notify resellers of material product changes with 30 days' advance notice where practical
  • ›Provide resellers with product roadmap updates quarterly

Getting Started as a Reseller

  • ›Initial discussion: Contact contact@verfix.ch with an overview of your customer base, target sectors, and intended reseller model.
  • ›Qualification: VerfiX will assess the reseller opportunity based on market fit, customer quality, and commercial potential.
  • ›Agreement: Review and execute the VerfiX Reseller Agreement.
  • ›Training: Complete VerfiX product training (available remotely).
  • ›Go to market: Begin offering VerfiX to your customer base.
