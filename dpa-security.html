Security & data-protection overview.

A summary of how VerfiX approaches security, privacy and data processing for regulated buyers. This overview supports procurement and due-diligence; a full DPA is provided on request.

Data protection

nFADP & GDPR

Retention

Security practices

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access control & audit logging
  • Append-only audit trail for every decision
  • Responsible-disclosure process
  • Principle of least privilege

Data Processing Agreement

A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) covering roles, sub-processors, security measures, data-subject rights and breach notification is available to customers and qualified prospects on request at info@verfix.ch.

This overview is informational and not a contract. VerfiX holds no FINMA, PCI or ISO certification at this stage; statements describe design intent, not certified controls.
