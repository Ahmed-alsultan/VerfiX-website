Identity Infrastructure for Swiss Banks

VerfiX for Swiss Banks

VerfiX provides Swiss banks with the identity and compliance infrastructure to onboard customers digitally, verify business clients, screen against AML lists, and maintain FINMA inspection-ready audit trails — with all processing in Switzerland.

The Challenge Swiss Banks Face in 2025

Swiss banks are navigating a confluence of pressures that make the current moment the most demanding in recent memory for compliance functions:

  • ›FATF grey listing: Switzerland's designation on the FATF grey list has triggered increased FINMA supervisory activity. Compliance programmes that were adequate in 2023 are now being scrutinised more rigorously.
  • ›Digital customer expectations: Retail and corporate customers expect fully digital onboarding. Banks that require in-person identification for account opening are losing customers to digital-first competitors.
  • ›Staff cost: Manual KYC and KYB processes consume significant compliance officer time. Growing volumes require growing headcount under a manual model.
  • ›Vendor data residency: Most global identity verification vendors process data outside Switzerland, creating compliance risk under nFADP and FINMA supervisory expectations.

VerfiX addresses all four pressures through a single Swiss-hosted platform.

VerfiX for Retail Banking

Digital Account Opening

Replace in-person or paper-based account opening with a fully digital process that complies with FINMA Circular 2016/7. Customers complete identity verification from their mobile device or computer in under three minutes. The bank receives a structured identity record with full audit trail immediately.

Telephone Banking Authentication

VerfiX Voice Biometrics provides FINMA-compliant authentication for telephone banking interactions. Customers are verified by their voice in seconds, supporting Swiss German, Standard German, French, Italian, and Romansh. Eliminates the vulnerability of password and PIN-based telephone authentication.

KYC Refresh

Periodically re-verify existing customers without requiring them to visit a branch. Send a digital KYC link, customer completes verification from their device, and the bank receives an updated audit record. High-risk customers can be refreshed annually at minimal operational cost.

VerfiX for Corporate and Private Banking

KYB and UBO Identification

For corporate account opening, VerfiX KYB automates the company registry lookup, ownership structure mapping, UBO identification, and director verification — tasks that currently take compliance analysts 2-3 days per corporate client. Each identified UBO is routed automatically through the KYC verification flow.

AML Programme Modernisation

VerfiX Trust Engine provides real-time AML screening at onboarding and continuous monitoring of the existing customer base. Configurable match thresholds, structured alert management, and full audit trail address the ongoing monitoring deficiency that FINMA most commonly identifies.

Swiss Data Residency — Non-Negotiable

Every Swiss bank we speak to has the same requirement: data must stay in Switzerland. VerfiX is the only identity verification platform purpose-built to meet this requirement. All processing and storage occurs in certified Swiss data centres. Our data processing agreement confirms Swiss residency. There are no carve-outs, no exceptions, and no cross-border transfers.

For cantonal banks subject to cantonal data protection requirements in addition to nFADP, Swiss data residency is the only acceptable option. VerfiX delivers it as standard.
