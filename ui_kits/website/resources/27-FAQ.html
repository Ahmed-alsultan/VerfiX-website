VerfiX — Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is VerfiX?

VerfiX AG is a Swiss identity infrastructure company. We provide a modular, API-first platform enabling regulated organisations to verify individuals (KYC), verify businesses (KYB), accept EU Digital Identity Wallet credentials (eID Gateway), and perform AML screening and risk scoring (Trust Engine). All processing occurs in Switzerland.

Who is VerfiX designed for?

VerfiX is designed for regulated organisations that are required by law or operational necessity to verify the identity of individuals or businesses. Primary customers include Swiss banks, fintechs, crypto platforms, insurance companies, telecom operators, government agencies, universities, and mobility providers.

Where is VerfiX based?

VerfiX AG is headquartered in Switzerland. All platform infrastructure is operated within Switzerland. Personal data processed through the VerfiX platform is never transferred outside Switzerland without explicit customer consent.

Compliance

Is VerfiX FINMA compliant?

VerfiX is designed to meet the requirements of FINMA Circular 2016/7 for remote video and online identification, including document verification, biometric face matching, and liveness detection. VerfiX also supports the GwG Art. 3 and Art. 4 identification and UBO identification obligations. Customers should confirm with their compliance counsel that VerfiX meets their specific regulatory requirements.

Is VerfiX nFADP compliant?

VerfiX is designed for nFADP compliance. Key features: all processing in Switzerland (satisfying data residency expectations); biometric data treated as sensitive personal data; Data Processing Agreement available for all customers; privacy by design built into platform architecture (biometric data not retained beyond session by default); configurable retention policies.

Does VerfiX provide a Data Processing Agreement?

Yes. VerfiX provides a standard DPA to all customers before they begin processing personal data through the Services. Enterprise customers may request customised DPA addenda. Contact privacy@verfix.ch.

What is Switzerland's FATF grey listing and how does it affect customers?

Switzerland was placed on the FATF grey list in late 2024, indicating identified deficiencies in AML enforcement. FINMA has responded with increased supervisory activity. For VerfiX customers, this means AML programme documentation quality is under greater scrutiny. VerfiX Trust Engine is designed to provide the structured, auditable AML programme that FINMA inspectors now expect.

Products

What documents does VerfiX KYC support?

VerfiX KYC supports more than 4,500 document types from 190+ countries, including passports (with NFC chip reading for ePassports), national identity cards, driving licences, residence permits, and visas. Swiss document types are fully supported including the Swiss passport, Swiss identity card, and Swiss driving licence.

What AML lists does VerfiX screen against?

VerfiX Trust Engine screens against SECO (Swiss national sanctions list), the UN Security Council consolidated list, the EU consolidated list, OFAC (US Treasury), HM Treasury (UK), and multiple PEP registries. Additional lists are available. The list selection is configurable per customer deployment.

What languages does VerfiX Voice Biometrics support?

VerfiX Voice Biometrics supports all four Swiss national languages: German (including Swiss German dialects), French, Italian, and Romansh.

What is VerfiX eID Gateway?

VerfiX eID Gateway enables Swiss organisations to accept EU Digital Identity Wallet credentials (eIDAS 2.0) from all 27 EU member states via a single API integration. Rather than building 27 separate integrations, customers integrate once with VerfiX Gateway and accept credentials from any EU national wallet.

Technical

How quickly can we integrate VerfiX?

Standard REST API integration typically takes 1-4 hours for a developer familiar with the documentation. Full enterprise integrations with custom workflows and multiple product modules typically take 1-2 business days. A sandbox environment is available within 24 hours of request.

What programming languages and frameworks does VerfiX support?

VerfiX is a REST API with JSON request and response format. It can be integrated from any programming language or framework that can make HTTPS requests. SDKs for Python, JavaScript, and Java are on the planned roadmap.

What deployment options are available?

VerfiX is available as a Swiss-hosted cloud service (recommended for most customers), private cloud (dedicated Swiss infrastructure), or on-premise (full deployment within the customer's own infrastructure). All deployment options maintain Swiss data residency.

How do webhooks work?

VerfiX sends a POST request to a registered endpoint URL when a specified event occurs (verification completed, AML hit, risk score generated, monitoring alert). Webhook payloads are signed with HMAC-SHA256. VerfiX retries webhook delivery on failure with exponential backoff.

Commercial

How is VerfiX priced?

VerfiX is priced on a per-successful-verification basis with volume tiers. Enterprise accounts may negotiate annual or monthly subscription pricing. Pricing is not published. Contact contact@verfix.ch for a custom quote based on your expected volumes and product requirements.

Is there a free trial or sandbox?

VerfiX provides a sandbox environment for all customers and prospective customers for integration testing. The sandbox is available within 24 hours of request and is not billed. Contact contact@verfix.ch to request sandbox access.

What support is provided?

VerfiX provides email support for all customers. Technical support during integration is included. Enterprise accounts receive dedicated support arrangements as specified in their Service Agreement.
