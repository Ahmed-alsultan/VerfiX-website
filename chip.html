The shift

Legacy ATM verification vs. VerfiX integrated ATM

Today: Legacy ATM

Cloud-dependent · PIN-based · high risk
  • Cloud dependent — biometric data sent to cloud increases risk and violates banking security policies.
  • PIN-based — PINs can be stolen, shared, shoulder-surfed, or brute-forced.
  • Fraud persists — card cloning, PIN theft, skimming, and social engineering continue.
  • High cost, low value — operational costs stay high while fraud losses and chargebacks persist.

Tomorrow: VerfiX ATM

On-premise · on-chip · secure by design
  • On-premise, on-chip — biometric data never leaves the ATM. Verification happens on the VerfiX chip.
  • Built for Swiss banks — aligned with nFADP & GDPR, data protection by design & default.
  • Stronger security — biometric + liveness detection stops fraud at the source. No PIN, no sharing, no cloning.
  • Higher ROI — lower fraud losses, reduced operational costs, higher customer trust and loyalty.
