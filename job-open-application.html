About the role

No open role that fits, but you believe you can help build European identity infrastructure? Tell us what you do best. We review every application and reach out when there is a match.

What you'll do

  • Tell us the kind of work you want to own and where you can add the most value.
  • Share examples of what you have built, shipped, or led.
  • Let us know your availability and preferred location/setup.

What we're looking for

  • Genuine interest in trust, identity, compliance, or deep-tech infrastructure.
  • A bias for ownership and clear communication.
  • Something concrete to show — code, writing, deals, or projects.

Nice to have

  • Experience in regulated industries or early-stage startups.
  • Swiss/EU base or work authorization.
  • Multilingual (EN/DE/FR).
