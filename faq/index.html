Identity verification confirms a person is who they claim to be. Swiss law — specifically GwG Art. 3 and FINMA Circular 2016/7 — requires financial intermediaries to verify customer identity before entering into a business relationship. Failure to comply exposes institutions to regulatory enforcement and reputational damage.

Full answer

FINMA Circular 2016/7 defines the technical requirements for remote video and online identification in Switzerland. It mandates document verification with security feature checks, biometric face matching, and liveness detection. VerfiX implements all requirements and generates a FINMA-compliant audit trail automatically.

KYC (Know Your Customer) requires financial intermediaries to identify customers, verify identity, and document the process under GwG Art. 3-8. FINMA Circular 2016/7 governs remote digital identification. Obligations include identification, documentation, and ten-year retention of all KYC records.

Full answer

Refresh frequency is risk-based: high-risk customers (PEPs, high-risk jurisdictions) annually; medium-risk every two to three years; standard-risk every three to five years. Event-triggered refresh is required on material changes regardless of schedule. VerfiX enables fully digital refresh without branch visits.
