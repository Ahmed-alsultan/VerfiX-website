Swiss Cantonal Bank

From three identity vendors to one API — with 82% less manual review

A Swiss cantonal bank operating across three cantons was running separate systems for document verification, AML screening, and customer due diligence refresh. Compliance teams spent 4–6 hours per day reconciling outputs from different vendors.

VerfiX replaced all three with a single REST API integration, unified audit trail, and automated AML rescreening. The bank went live in 11 days.

"We evaluated four vendors. VerfiX was the only one that could handle Swiss document types, meet our FINMA audit trail requirements, and deploy within our existing infrastructure in under two weeks."

— Head of Digital Compliance, Swiss Cantonal Bank

Fintech · Switzerland

Swiss Payment Platform

Merchant onboarding from 3 days to 4 hours — with automated KYB and sanctions screening

A Swiss payment platform processing over CHF 200M annually needed to scale merchant onboarding without scaling their compliance team. Manual KYB checks on each new merchant took an average of 3 days and required two full-time analysts.

VerfiX automated the full KYB flow: company registry lookup, UBO identification, director ID verification, and real-time AML screening. Analysts now review only flagged cases.

"Our compliance team was the bottleneck for growth. VerfiX automated the 80% of onboardings that were straightforward and let our analysts focus on the edge cases that actually need human judgment."

— VP Compliance, Swiss Payment Platform
