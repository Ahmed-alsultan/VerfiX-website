VerfiX
Company Fact Sheet

Swiss identity & payment infrastructure.

VerfiX unifies identity verification, business verification, AML screening, fraud intelligence and trust scoring into one Swiss-made platform — deployable on-premise and on-chip, so sensitive biometric data never leaves the device.

What we do

KYC / KYB

Document, face & voice biometrics, liveness, video, AML and business verification in one flow.

Gateway

One API to accept trusted European digital identity wallets (eIDAS 2.0).

VerfiX Chip

On-device face & voice verification for ATMs, POS and access points.

Trust Engine™

Every signal fused into one explainable, configurable trust decision.

Why now

eIDAS 2.0

EU digital identity wallets rolling out toward 2026.

Swiss nFADP

Revised data-protection law in force since Sept 2023.

Swiss e-ID Act

Federal e-ID approved 2024; state e-ID planned.
