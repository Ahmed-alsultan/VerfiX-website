Non-European

Privacy-exposed

Costly

VerfiX · 05Product
Flagship MVP

VerfiX Chip — pay with your face and voice.

A Swiss-made secure element that verifies face and voice on the device itself — the foundation for cardless, sovereign, privacy-first payments at the ATM and POS.

  • Authorize a withdrawal or payment with a glance and a phrase — no card, no PIN.
  • Biometric templates are processed and matched on the chip — never sent to a cloud.
  • Swiss data residency and nFADP / GDPR alignment by design.
  • Built for banks, ATMs and POS terminals across regulated industries.
