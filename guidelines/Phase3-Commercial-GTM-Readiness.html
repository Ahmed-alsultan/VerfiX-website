4 · Sales operating system

Pipeline & playbook

Lead pipeline
Prospect→Qualified→Discovery→Demo→Proposal→Pilot→Customer

Frameworks

  • Discovery — current flow, volumes, pain, compliance owner, timeline.
  • Demo — run the live console + Trust Engine on the prospect's scenario.
  • Proposal — scoped quote + deployment model + pilot plan.
  • Pilot conversion — review vs success metrics → Enterprise plan.

CRM structure

  • Account → opportunity → pilot → subscription.
  • Stage gates aligned to the pipeline above.
  • Required fields: industry, volume band, checks, deployment, compliance owner.

Playbook is a framework here — wire into your CRM of choice (HubSpot/Pipedrive/Salesforce).

6 · Investor narrative & GTM

Story & market entry

Investor narrative

  • Problem: regulated onboarding is fragmented, vendor-locked and opaque.
  • Solution: a Swiss-native orchestration + decision layer (Trust Engine™).
  • Why now: tightening EU/CH compliance + demand for sovereign data.
  • Moat: orchestration neutrality + Swiss trust brand + future marketplace.
  • Vision: European identity infrastructure (Identity Gateway, Compliance Hub).

Financial assumptions intentionally not numericized here — model with real volume/pricing inputs, not invented figures.

GTM — markets & ICP

  • Markets: CH first → DE, FR, AT (DACH + France).
  • Segments: banking, fintech, crypto, insurance, telecom, government.
  • ICP: regulated business, live onboarding volume, named compliance owner, integration capacity.
  • Motion: founder-led discovery → live demo → fixed-fee pilot → land & expand.
  • Entry wedge: the 30/60/90 pilot de-risks the first deployment.
