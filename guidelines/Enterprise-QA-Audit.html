What's still missing to read as enterprise-grade

  • Depth over breadth. The brand and shell already look enterprise; the gap is product surface depth — the workflows a buyer's evaluation team clicks through (rules, KYB, cases).
  • Developer credibility. A real docs portal with sidebar nav, search, language tabs and a sandbox is the single highest-leverage addition.
  • Proof & transparency. A live trust/compliance center (certs, sub-processors, DPA, residency map) converts regulated buyers.
  • Swiss localization. DE/FR/IT language switching is an expectation, not a nicety, in this market.
