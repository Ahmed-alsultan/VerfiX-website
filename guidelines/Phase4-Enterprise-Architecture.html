Inside your infrastructure. Data never leaves your perimeter.
5 · Trust Engine™ & Workflow Engine

Scoring and routing

Trust Engine inputs → outputs

Signals in: OCR, face match, liveness, AML, KYB, device, fraud. Out: 0–100 score, risk tier, decision — with an explainability record naming the contributing signals.

  • Weighted, configurable per policy (Rules Builder).
  • Every score change is traceable + regulator-explainable.
  • Deterministic in sandbox for testing.
