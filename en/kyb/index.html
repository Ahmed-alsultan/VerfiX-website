Frequently Asked Questions about KYB

Everything about VerfiX KYB — business verification process, Swiss regulation and UBO identification.

KYB (Know Your Business) is business verification — the equivalent of KYC identification for legal entities. While KYC verifies an individual's identity, KYB verifies legal entities (companies, foundations, trusts) and their Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs).

KYB typically covers: company registry entry, corporate structure, directors and board members, UBOs (>25% ownership), AML screening of the legal entity and key persons, and sanctions/PEP checks.

Yes. The Swiss Anti-Money Laundering Act (GwG) requires supervised institutions to identify the contracting party and to determine the beneficial owner for legal entities (GwG Art. 4). VerfiX KYB fully meets these requirements: company registry verification, automatic UBO determination down to the natural person, AML screening against SECO, UN, EU, OFAC lists, and full audit trail for FINMA inspections.

Swiss GwG Art. 3 & 4FINMA compliant

Duration depends on corporate structure complexity:

  • Simple structure (direct owner, Swiss registry entry): 2–10 minutes fully automated
  • Medium complexity (multiple owners, holding structure): 10–30 minutes
  • Complex structures (international holdings, trusts, foundations): 1–4 hours with partial manual review

In all cases significantly faster than manual processes, which typically take 2–5 business days.

VerfiX KYB accesses official data sources: Switzerland (Zefix, cantonal commercial registries), EU (national registries via BRIS network), International (partner databases for 180+ countries), Sanctions lists (SECO, UN Consolidated, EU Consolidated, OFAC SDN, OFSI), Global PEP databases (updated daily), and Adverse Media screening.

VerfiX KYB automatically determines UBOs: ownership structure analysis down to natural persons holding >25% (FATF standard), penetration of holding structures and SPVs, automatic KYC screening of identified individuals (sanctions, PEP, adverse media), and documentation of the full ownership chain as an auditable record.

Yes. VerfiX KYB is available via the same REST API as all other VerfiX products. Typical integration time: 1–3 weeks. Batch processing for high volumes, webhook status notifications, white-label onboarding flows, and automatic periodic re-verification (Refresh KYB) are all available.

Negative results trigger configurable workflows: sanctions hit — immediate transaction block and compliance team notification (with SECO reporting obligation check); PEP match — escalation to management approval and Enhanced Due Diligence workflow; Adverse media — risk assessment and manual review routing. All decisions are documented in the audit log with full justification.
