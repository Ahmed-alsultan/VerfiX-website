Tiny uppercase status badge. Tone is semantic — pick by meaning, not looks.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success">Verified</Badge>
<Badge tone="warn">Pending</Badge>
<Badge tone="danger">Rejected</Badge>
<Badge tone="info">KYB</Badge>
<Badge tone="red" icon="fas fa-bolt">New</Badge>
```

Tones: `success` (verified/approved), `warn` (review/pending), `danger` (rejected/flagged), `info` (categorical), `red`/`neutral` (labels). Use sparingly — one badge per row.
