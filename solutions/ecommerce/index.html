Industry Challenges

E-commerce faces identity risk at checkout and beyond

From age-restricted products to high-value fraud to marketplace AML obligations, e-commerce identity challenges span the full transaction lifecycle.

Age verification for regulated products

Swiss and EU regulations require age verification for alcohol, tobacco, gambling, adult content, and other age-restricted products. Manual age checks at delivery are costly and unreliable. Digital age verification at checkout must be reliable, fast, and privacy-compliant.

Payment fraud on high-value transactions

Card-not-present fraud peaks for high-value purchases — luxury goods, electronics, jewellery. Identity verification at checkout for transactions above risk threshold significantly reduces authorised push payment fraud and chargeback exposure.

Marketplace seller and buyer identity

Marketplace platforms face AML and fraud obligations at both sides of the transaction. Sellers offering fraudulent goods, buyers using stolen payment instruments, and professional fraudsters operating multiple accounts all require identity controls at account creation and transaction level.

Account takeover and credential stuffing

E-commerce accounts with stored payment methods, loyalty points, and purchase history are valuable attack targets. Credential stuffing attacks using stolen username/password combinations are automated and high-volume.

AML obligations for high-value luxury and marketplace sales

Swiss luxury goods sellers and marketplace operators above certain thresholds face AML obligations under GwG — requiring KYC for high-value cash or electronic transactions. Non-compliance exposes merchants to supervisory risk.

Abandonment from friction at verification

Every additional step at checkout reduces conversion. Poorly designed identity verification creates unacceptable abandonment rates. The verification must be genuinely fast — sub-three-minute at most — and optimised for mobile completion.

The VerfiX Approach

Identity verification that converts — not one that abandons

VerfiX is designed for the e-commerce context: fast, mobile-optimised, and invisible to the customer journey for low-risk transactions.

Age verification — seamless and legally compliant

For age-restricted products, VerfiX verifies the buyer's age at checkout through document verification — the buyer photographs their ID or passport, VerfiX extracts the date of birth, and confirms the buyer is above the required age threshold. Processing under two seconds. Result embedded in the checkout flow without redirect.

Transaction risk-based identity — friction only when needed

VerfiX integrates with payment and risk systems to trigger identity verification only for transactions that meet risk criteria — above a value threshold, new delivery address, first transaction with a payment method. Low-risk transactions proceed without additional steps. High-risk transactions trigger a quick biometric check.

Marketplace seller KYC — GwG and platform compliance

Marketplace platforms use VerfiX to KYC sellers at registration — document verification, biometric matching, and AML screening. For business sellers, the KYB flow verifies the company and identifies the beneficial owner. This protects the platform from hosting fraudulent sellers and meets marketplace regulatory obligations.

Account takeover prevention — biometric login option

VerfiX provides biometric authentication for account recovery and high-value account actions — adding a payment method, changing shipping address, requesting a refund above threshold. Biometric authentication is stronger than password plus SMS 2FA against account takeover attacks.

AML screening for luxury and high-value sales

For high-value transactions subject to GwG, VerfiX provides real-time AML screening integrated into the transaction approval flow. The buyer's verified identity is checked against SECO and other lists before the transaction completes. The AML screening result is retained in the transaction audit trail.

Privacy-compliant age verification — no data retention

Age verification requires confirmation of age, not storage of the buyer's identity document. VerfiX extracts only the date of birth and the age verification result — the document image and biometric data are not retained post-session. Buyers confirm age without creating a permanent identity record.

Business Benefits

Measurable outcomes from day one

Age verification compliance without abandonment

Sub-two-second age checks embedded in the checkout flow achieve compliance with age restriction laws without materially increasing checkout abandonment. Integration using VerfiX typically adds under 30 seconds to the checkout journey for age-verified products.

Chargeback and fraud losses reduced

Biometric identity verification for high-risk transactions — above value threshold, new delivery address, first-use payment methods — significantly reduces authorised push payment fraud, chargeback claims, and the operational cost of fraud dispute management.

Marketplace fraud and AML risk managed

KYC at seller registration closes the primary vector for marketplace fraud — anonymous seller accounts. AML screening for high-value transactions meets GwG obligations and protects against marketplace use for money laundering.

Account takeover attempts neutralised

Biometric authentication for high-risk account actions makes credential stuffing attacks ineffective. Stolen passwords cannot override biometric verification. Account takeover losses are reduced and the customer experience on genuine account recovery is improved.

Privacy-compliant identity — no permanent customer data

Age verification without data retention satisfies consumer privacy expectations and GDPR/nFADP data minimisation. Customers are not creating a permanent identity profile for a one-time age check — a material differentiator for consumer trust.

Conversion optimised — friction only where needed

Risk-based triggering means low-risk transactions see zero additional friction. The 95%+ of clean transactions proceed without interruption. Verification is triggered selectively where risk signals justify it — protecting revenue while managing fraud.

Verification Workflow

From request to verified result in seconds

VerfiX slots into your existing stack. No rearchitecting. One REST API call returns document data, biometric result, AML screening outcome, and a FINMA-compliant audit record.

01

Customer adds age-restricted product or high-value item to cart

The checkout system identifies the product as age-restricted or the transaction as above the risk-threshold. The VerfiX API is called to initiate the appropriate verification flow.

02

Customer completes quick verification

For age verification: customer photographs ID and VerfiX extracts date of birth — under two seconds. For full identity verification: document + selfie in under three minutes. Flow is embedded in checkout without redirect.

03

Verification result returned to checkout

VerfiX returns: age confirmed / identity verified, verification confidence, and audit record identifier. For AML-required transactions, the AML screening result is included. The checkout system proceeds or holds based on the result.

04

Transaction completed — audit trail stored

Approved transactions complete normally. The verification audit record is stored in Swiss infrastructure. For GwG-required AML documentation, the record is retained for the required period.

05

Post-transaction fraud monitoring

For marketplace sellers, ongoing AML monitoring continues after onboarding. New sanctions matches trigger webhook alerts. The audit trail supports chargeback dispute documentation when required.
