About VerfiX

Built in Switzerland for regulated trust infrastructure.

VerfiX is a Swiss trust infrastructure platform serving banks, fintechs, insurers, crypto companies, healthcare providers, and governments across Switzerland and Europe.

We exist because regulated organizations deserve a complete trust infrastructure layer — not a collection of fragmented point solutions. One platform, one API, one decision engine.

Headquartered in Switzerland

Swiss-incorporated. Swiss regulatory requirements built in from day one.

Compliance-First Design

nFADP, GDPR, and sector-specific requirements inform every product decision.

API-First Engineering

REST APIs, SDKs, webhooks, and developer documentation for fast integration.

Leadership

A focused team with deep expertise in identity infrastructure, compliance engineering, and regulated technology.
